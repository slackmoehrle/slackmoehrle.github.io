Introduction

**

Making a Dr. Mario style game in Cocos Creator

Dr. Mario was published by Nintendo in the early 1990's. It turned out to be one of the most popular games of all time. In case you are not familiar you can watch some game play. For a history lesson check out Wikipedia and this article about how the game was prototpyed.

You can download the complete game, however it is recommended that you follow this tutorial step by step for maximum understanding.
